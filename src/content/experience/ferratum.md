## Job role

Ferratum is an international provider of mobile consumer loans. I worked as senior software developer, team lead, and Lead Scrum Master — driving the agile transformation that coached the entire development team through the methodology change. This was my first leadership role, and it centered on developer enablement: I mentored developers transitioning to Scrum, established coaching rhythms, and built the CI/CD pipeline that accelerated delivery cycles.

Leading a distributed team across offices in Bratislava and Amsterdam, I hired and mentored new developers, defined the team's technical strategy, and ensured consistent engineering practices across locations. I also led the team that implemented security for the first Ferratum bank in Malta — a high-stakes project requiring cross-functional coordination and regulatory awareness. Beyond internal team development, I traveled to company offices in several countries, building relationships and aligning engineering practices across cultures. The agile transformation I led improved communication, shortened development cycles, and raised software quality across the organization.
