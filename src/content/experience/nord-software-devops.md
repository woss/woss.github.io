## Job role

Multi-client engagement as Senior Developer and DevOps at Nord Software, working across multiple projects in diverse industries — a consulting model that required deep client understanding and cross-team collaboration. At GameRefinery (SaaS platform for mobile game analysis) I was lead in backend development and data modeling. Adoveo/Ratataa (interactive video advertising) gave me insights into online advertising and video streaming, where I owned backend development and infrastructure. This is when I discovered Docker and Packer, and started introducing them to client projects. I also worked with Dovre Group (project management services in oil business) on implementing and maintaining features and services. Notable engagements included RapidCampaign/NearMe (backend with Symfony2) and Limiitti (backend with Yii). Across every client engagement, I learned to translate business needs into technical solutions — a consultative skill that's central to developer relations and ecosystem work.
