## Job role

I joined Adria24 as a junior software developer. I worked on the development of their main application and several satellite sites, gaining experience with databases, APIs, and frontend technologies. This was my first real-world experience in software development, and it sparked my passion for building web applications. Adria24 was one of 10 biggest Croatian travel agencies including over 100 satellite sites of specialized destinations across the Croatian coastline. Websites powered by a custom CMS connected to a booking system with VOIP integration. I was responsible for maintaining and improving the existing codebase, as well as developing new features and optimizing performance. This role allowed me to gain valuable experience in software development, project management, and working in a team environment. It also laid the foundation for my future career in software development and entrepreneurship.
